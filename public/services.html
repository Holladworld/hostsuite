How Businesses Structure Their Web Presence

An evaluation of standard tactical paths compared directly against HostSuite active monitoring.

1. In-House Management

Assigning regular internal employees to update web pages alongside day-to-day work tasks.

Pros

Uses internal staff; clear, immediate oversight on specific localized brand announcements.

Cons

Employees rarely have specialized system backend configurations domain expertise; fixes stall during large campaigns.

2. One-Time Freelancers

Contracting individual project freelancers via remote platforms for single, isolated adjustments.

Pros

Pay strictly for defined project scopes; clean operational bounds for simple fixes.

Cons

Zero active system monitoring; variable quality; high friction rehiring or debugging past work during unexpected server downfalls.
